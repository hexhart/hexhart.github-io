Our Story

Founded in 2024, in a neighborhood where three bestfriends whom were pet lovers resided, Alex, Sarah, and Mark. Getting weary from the demands of traditional pet ownership that includes vet visits, spending expensive vet bills, pet foods, cutesy accessories, cleaning up after their furry friends, and ultimately how short lived their beloved pets are. With the latter, they longed for a solution.

One evening, during a game night, Alex proposed an idea: robotic companions that could offer the same love without the hassle. Excited by the concept, they poured their hearts into creating BuddyBots, lifelike pet robots that offered companionship and assistance. As a team, they worked together to form a startup company, BuddyBot Innovations. Worry less, their pets are still with them and getting the love they deserve.
